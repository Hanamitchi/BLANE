FEATURES

9 nutrition modules

Every module works together to give you a complete, adaptive, and explainable nutrition experience.

1
Module 01
Real-Time Body Feedback Loop

Continuously syncs with wearable and health data to adjust your nutrition targets on the fly — no more static meal plans.

2
Module 02
Health Drift Detection

Detects subtle shifts in your health metrics over time and proactively recalibrates your recommendations before issues arise.

3
Module 03
Dynamic Portion Optimizer

Computes ideal portion sizes based on your activity level, metabolic rate, and daily goals — recalculated every day.

4
Module 04
GeoMarket Ingredient Scanner

Scans nearby markets and stores for available ingredients so that every meal plan is realistic and locally actionable.

5-6
Module 05 & 06
Price-Aware & Seasonal Optimizer

Combines real-time local pricing data with seasonal ingredient availability to suggest meals that are not just healthy, but genuinely affordable and easy to source in your area right now. Never recommends out-of-season produce at peak prices.

7
Module 07
Explainable AI

Every recommendation comes with a clear, human-readable reason — so you always understand why BLANE suggests what it does.

8
Module 08
Constraint-Based Planning

Handles allergies, intolerances, religious restrictions, and dietary preferences as hard constraints — never violated.

9
Module 09
Recipe Recommendation

Matches your available ingredients and nutritional needs with a curated, step-by-step recipe library tailored to your skill level.
